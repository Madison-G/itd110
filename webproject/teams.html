NWHL Teams

Connecticut Whale

The Connecticut Whale are one of the founding four teams in the league. They are the only team to not have won the Isobel Cup. The Whale's color combination are in respect to the former NHL team called the Hartford Whalers.


Boston Pride

The Boston Pride are one of the founding four teams. They won the Isobel Cup in 2016, the year after they were founded. They are the best team in the league being undefeated in 7 games.


Buffalo Beauts

The Buffalo Beauts are one of the founding four teams. The Beauts won the Isobel Cup in 2017 against the Boston Pride. The Beauts have made it to the Isobel Cup Finals every year since their existence.


Metropolitian Riveters

The Metropolitian Riveters are one of the founding four teams. Their logo and name are based off Rosie the Riveter. They won the Isobel Cup in 2018 against the Beauts.


Minnesota Whitecaps

The Minnesota Whitecaps were not in the founding four, but they have been a team since 2004. They were bought by the NWHL and officially joined the league in 2018. They have won the Clarkson Cup in 2010 and the Isobel Cup in 2019.
